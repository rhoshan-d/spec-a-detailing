About Us

About Our Team

Spec-A Detailing brings together experts passionate about car care. Our team provides professional valeting services across Kildare and surrounding areas.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Spec-A Detailing was born out of a passion for immaculate vehicles and a desire to offer convenient, professional car care services across Ireland. What started as a small mobile operation has grown into one of Kildare's most trusted car valeting businesses.

Our team of experienced professionals are committed to the highest standards of auto detailing, using only the finest products and techniques to ensure your vehicle receives the care it deserves.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional mobile car valeting and detailing services that exceed our customers' expectations, while respecting their time and the environment.

Why Choose Spec-A Detailing?

Mobile Convenience

We come to your home or workplace, saving you valuable time and hassle. Our fully equipped mobile units bring the detailing shop to you.

Professional Expertise

Our team is trained in the latest detailing techniques and technologies. We regularly update our skills to provide the best service possible.

Eco-Friendly Approach

We're committed to using environmentally responsible products and practices, minimizing water usage and reducing our carbon footprint.
